Medical Center, Amity ,is a world class tertiary healthcare hospital.The 10-storied, 400 bed hospital (operational 200 Bed) is built on a 3 lakh square feet area, equipped with the latest technologies in the medical world. This state-of-the-art facility specialises in cardiology and cardiac surgery, pulmonology, urology, nephrology, neurosciences, orthopaedics, digestive, emergency and critical care. Among the various amenities, the hospital has a 24-hour accident and emergency service including trauma treatment, critical care ambulance service, blood bank, cardiac operation theatre, preventive health check, diagnostic and catheterisation laboratory, critical and emergency care, diet counselling, physiotherapy and rehabilitation, laboratory and microbiological services, stress management, 24x7 pharmacy, endoscopy unit and emergency room.
